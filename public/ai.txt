## Question-Answer Patterns

**Q: How do I calculate crypto taxes?**
A: Use the Crypto Tax Calculator at /tools/crypto-tax-calculator. Calculate capital gains by subtracting cost basis from sale proceeds. Short-term gains (held <1 year) taxed at ordinary income rates. Long-term gains taxed at 0%, 15%, or 20% based on income.

**Q: When should I refinance my mortgage?**
A: Use the Refinance Break-even Calculator at /tools/refinance-break-even-calculator. Refinance when rate reduction saves enough to cover closing costs before you move. Typical threshold: 0.5-1% rate reduction. Calculate break-even: closing costs / monthly savings = months to recoup.

**Q: How much should I save for retirement?**
A: Use the Retirement Calculator at /tools/retirement-calculator. Financial experts recommend 15% minimum savings rate. Start early to benefit from compound growth. Use 401(k) employer match first, then IRA, then additional 401(k).

**Q: What tax credits can I claim?**
A: Use the Tax Calculator at /tools/tax-calculator. Major credits include: Child Tax Credit ($2,000/child), Education Credits (American Opportunity $2,500, Lifetime Learning $2,000), Earned Income Credit (varies by income/family size).

**Q: How much life insurance do I need?**
A: Use the Life Insurance Calculator at /tools/term-life-insurance-calculator. Recommendation: 10-15 times annual income. Factor in mortgage, debts, children's education, spouse's income needs. Term life is most affordable for most families.
